About me

My name is Bryan Wise, and I am a software engineer from Florida. Most of my experience lies in full-stack development. I am proficient with development standards such as Object-Oriented Design, MVC, MERN Stacks, Agile Project Management, etc.

I have always been interested in how things function, and this field of work satiates that curiosity and drives me to find exceptional solutions for my customers. I dive into projects with a relentless drive to enhance them—to make them great.

When I'm not coding or researching new technology, you can find me playing in a local soccer league or trying to master the technology of the kitchen (looking at you Ninja Foodi). My curiosity about our galaxy and outer space is also one of my personal passions.

Feel free to browse my portfolio, as I believe it scratches the surface of who I am as a developer. Thank you for your consideration.
